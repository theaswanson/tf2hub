TF2 Center tf2center.com

TF2Center was created to help bridge the gap between public and competitive play for TF2. TF2Center is a place to play TF2 games based on specific competitive game-modes like 6v6 and Highlander. Newer players can take advantage of our lobby system to create games without needing to get a server personally, whilst more experienced players can ensure games are competitive by imposing restrictions.
